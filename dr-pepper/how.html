There are 3 ways to participate for a chance to win†:

Submit your Video

Dr Pepper is back with a contest to help YOU enrich your education!

Submit an original video (See Official Rule 4b for full details) telling us how you want to enrich your education for a chance to win $20,000 in Tuition Cash! After the Contest Closes, all videos will be judged by our Judges.

To submit your video:

  1. Register for the contest, or sign in if you have already registered
  2. Upload your original video on to our website on the Home tab
    • Remember, videos must:
      • Be either .mp4 or .mov format
      • Be no more than one (1) minute long
      • Be your original creation
      • Be submitted in either English or French

Play With Pins

Users get one play with every unique PIN entered for the chance to win Instant Prizes and earn an automatic entry into the Weekly Prize draw!

To play “Match to Win” online:

  1. Purchase a specially-marked Dr Pepper product to obtain a unique 8-digit PIN code located under the cap or inside cartons
  2. Register for the contest, or sign in if you have already registered
  3. Enter your PIN to play for a chance to WIN
    • Play the Match to Win game for your chance to win instantly
    • Plus, you will be given an automatic entry into that week’s draw for a trip for 2 to Mexico or the Dominican Republic
  4. Remember to come back every time you obtain a unique PIN located on specially-marked Dr Pepper products (while supplies last).

For complete details, visit the Full Contest Rules and Regulations.
