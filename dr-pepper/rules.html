    • Number of vote points will be awarded as follows: Based on the total number of eligible video entries received during the Contest Period the Contest Sponsor will perform the following calculation in order to award the defined number of points to each entrant who received a minimum of one (1) vote during the Contest Period.
    • Calculation is based on the following: The Contest Sponsor will take 10% of the total number of entries received during the Contest Period representing by X below. (i.e. 500 entries received during the Contest Period x 10% = 50 entries) and award the following points to each entrant. Therefore:
    • The first X number of entries, who received the highest number of overall eligible votes, will be awarded 15 points.
    • The next X entries who received the next overall highest number of eligible votes will be award 10 points.
    • The next X entries who received the next overall highest number of eligible votes, will be awarded 5 points.
    • All remaining entries who received a minimum of one (1) vote will receive 2 points.
    • In the event that the first or more entries after the X number have the same number of votes as the last entry, those entries will be awarded the same amount of points as the last entry allowing for more than X entries for each set of points. Once the number of votes changes, the Contest Sponsor will begin to count the next X entries. This process will be repeated for each number of points awarded if necessary.
